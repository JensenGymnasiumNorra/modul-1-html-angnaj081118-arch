Banana pancakes

Banana pancakes rich in potassium and very simple. They are naturally sweet,very easy to make and very delicious,along with alot of potassium..


Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup mashed overripe banana. about 1 ½ large
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ cup milk
  • Banana sliced (for serving)
  • Maple syrup (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. In another, larger bowl, beat the banana and egg together until blended. Whisk in the milk until combined, then whisk in the dry ingredients. The batter will be slightly lumpy.
  3. Using a ⅓-cup measuring cup, pour 3-4 circles of batter into the pan.
  4. Cook until the pancakes are puffed on top and golden brown on the bottom, 2-3 minutes, then flip and cook on the other side until risen and cooked through, 1-2 minutes more. Makes 8 pancakes.
  5. Serve with banana slices and maple syrup.
