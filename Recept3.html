Fried bananas

Fried bananas,lots of potassium and very simple. They are naturally sweet and extra sweet from the brown sugar too,very easy to make and very delightful,along with lots of potassium..


Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 tablespoons oil or butter
  • 2 tablespoons Water
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Peel the bananas and slice crosswise into ½ inch rounds.
  2. In a nonstick skillet over medium heat, melt butter or heat oil. Stir in water, honey, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt until combined.
  3. Add banana slices in a single layer.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, flipping carefully with a spatula, until browned and caramelized on both sides. If browning too quickly, turn the heat down to prevent burning. Bananas should be golden brown when done.
  5. Serve warm banana slices drizzled with the delicious pan juices.
